# NAVI, The Digital Omen, and The Alchemist

Remember the story of Santiago, the shepherd boy in *The Alchemist*?  
He traveled the world in search of a treasure, guided only by **Omens**—signals from the universe.

Paulo Coelho wrote, *"When you want something, all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it."*

I’ve always been fascinated by that idea. The notion that hidden messages are waiting for us, if only we know how to read them.

So I built a digital **Omen**
